Car design
 

Ferrari is using AWS to run car performance simulations of various racing and driving conditions using high-performance computing (HPC). Ferrari can run thousands of simulations to gain insights faster than running simulations, encouraging experimentation and innovation in design. The modelling starts from reconstructing the tyre forces from the available telemetry signals that we use in in-car camera images (accelerometers, speed sensors, etc). We then fit a parametric tyre model to the data and from this we can calculate the maximum force that each tyre is capable of generating in all the various car conditions. click here for more info

Fan engagement
 

Scuderia Ferrari will use technologies like compute and media services to build a new digital fan engagement platform via a mobile app. The app will deliver a fully personalized experience that informs, educates, and entertains fans about one of the most storied teams in racing history. Take fan engagement to the next level with AWS, sports organizations can capture first-party data, promote and monetize experiences, and activate solutions. Better consumer interactions
 

Ferrari will be able to quickly create, deploy, and scale digital experiences for Ferrari enthusiasts. Customers will be able to custom-build their car and immerse themselves in high-resolution visualizations, and also manage an owner's service and maintenance information through Ferrari's Vehicle Information Hub. F1 Insights add a new real-time dimension to statistics and place context around race data to help fans better appreciate key moments on the track. More than 300 sensors on each race car generate over 1.1 million data points per second that F1 transmits from the cars to the pit and onto AWS for processing. click here for more info
